About us

Wildlife Reserves Singapore (WRS) is dedicated to the management of world-leading zoological institutions—Jurong Bird Park, Night Safari, River Safari and Singapore Zoo—that aim to inspire people to value and conserve biodiversity by providing meaningful and memorable wildlife experiences. A self-funded organisation, WRS focuses on protecting biodiversity in Singapore and Southeast Asia through collaborations with like-minded partners, organisations and institutions. Each year, the four attractions welcome 4.6 million visitors.

Get involved

For many years, Wildlife Reserves Singapore (WRS) has actively played a part in animal conservation efforts globally. Through these projects, many endangered and threatened species can have a fighting chance in ensuring their survival for the next generation and beyond.
